Welcome to Nopaninos Preschool

Your child’s journey to joyful learning and discovery starts here. Safe, fun, and nurturing environment tailored for your little one.

About Nopaninos

We focus on creating a safe and stimulating environment where children aged 2 to 6 grow through play, exploration, and guided learning. Our experienced educators inspire creativity and build strong foundations for lifelong learning.

Our Programmes

Play Group

Engaging toddlers aged 2-3 with fun learning and play activities for early development.

Nursery

Stimulating early childhood development with creative programs for ages 3-4 years.

Lower KG

Preparing children aged 4-5 with foundational skills to excel in Senior KG.

Upper KG

Building strong literacy and numeracy foundations for ages 5-6 to prepare for formal schooling.
